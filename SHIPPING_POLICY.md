# Shipping & Delivery Policy

**Last Updated**: November 13, 2024

## Overview

TrustPay is a **digital escrow service platform** that facilitates secure UPI payments between parties. As we provide digital financial services and do not sell or ship physical products, this policy clarifies our service delivery model.

### 1.1 Digital Service

- We provide escrow services for UPI payments
- **No physical products** are sold or shipped by TrustPay
- All services are delivered electronically

### 1.2 What We Provide
- Secure escrow account creation
- UPI payment processing
- Transaction monitoring
- Dispute resolution services
- Digital transaction receipts
- Blockchain-based transaction proofs

## 3. Third-Party Goods/Services

### 3.1 Escrow for Physical Goods
While TrustPay doesn't ship products, users may use our escrow service for transactions involving physical goods between third parties.

**Important Notes**:
- TrustPay is **not responsible** for shipping or delivery of goods
- Shipping is arranged between **buyer and seller** directly
- TrustPay only holds payment until delivery is confirmed
- We do not track shipments or handle logistics

### 3.2 User Responsibilities

**Seller Responsibilities**:
- Arrange shipping/delivery of goods
- Provide tracking information to buyer
- Ensure timely delivery as agreed
- Provide proof of delivery when requested

**Buyer Responsibilities**:
- Provide accurate delivery address to seller
- Track shipment independently
- Confirm receipt of goods on TrustPay platform
- Raise disputes if goods not received

---

## 4. Delivery Confirmation

### 4.1 For Digital Services
- Services delivered instantly upon escrow creation
- Confirmation email sent immediately
- Transaction visible in dashboard in real-time

### 4.2 For Third-Party Goods (Using Our Escrow)
- Buyer must confirm receipt of goods on platform
- Confirmation triggers fund release to seller
- If no confirmation within agreed timeframe, buyer can raise dispute
- Seller should provide delivery proof (tracking, photos, etc.)

## 5. Delivery Timeline (Third-Party Goods)

### 5.1 Standard Timeline
The delivery timeline for goods purchased using TrustPay escrow depends entirely on the agreement between buyer and seller:

| Type | Typical Timeline |
|------|------------------|
| Local delivery | 1-3 days |
| Domestic shipping | 3-7 days |
| Express delivery | 1-2 days |
| Digital goods/services | Instant to 24 hours |

**Note**: TrustPay does not control or guarantee these timelines.

### 5.2 Escrow Hold Period
- Funds held in escrow until delivery confirmed
- Default hold period: 7 days (customizable)
- Can be extended by mutual agreement
- Auto-release if no dispute raised within agreed period

---

## 6. Delivery Issues (Third-Party Goods)

### 6.1 Non-Delivery
If goods are not delivered:
1. Buyer raises dispute on TrustPay platform
2. Seller has 3-5 days to respond with proof
3. TrustPay reviews evidence
4. Funds refunded to buyer if non-delivery confirmed

### 6.2 Delayed Delivery
- Buyer and seller should communicate directly
- Escrow hold period can be extended
- If excessive delay, buyer can raise dispute
- Refund processed if seller cannot fulfill

### 6.3 Damaged/Wrong Items
- Buyer should document with photos/videos
- Raise dispute with evidence
- Seller can offer replacement or refund
- TrustPay facilitates resolution
