# Cancellation & Refunds Policy

**Last Updated**: November 13, 2024

## Overview

TrustPay is an escrow service platform that facilitates secure UPI payments between parties. This policy outlines the cancellation and refund procedures for transactions made through our platform.

---

## 1. Escrow Transaction Cancellation

### 1.1 Before Payment
- Users can cancel an escrow transaction **before making the UPI payment** without any charges
- Simply close the payment request or let it expire (typically 15 minutes)
- No refund is necessary as no payment has been made

### 1.2 After Payment (Funds in Escrow)
- Once payment is made and funds are held in escrow, cancellation requires **mutual agreement** from both parties (payer and payee)
- If both parties agree to cancel:
  - Full refund will be processed to the payer's UPI account
  - Processing time: 3-5 business days
  - TrustPay service fee (if applicable) is **non-refundable**

### 1.3 Unilateral Cancellation
- If only one party wants to cancel, the other party must agree
- If no agreement is reached, either party can raise a **dispute**
- Disputes are resolved through our dispute resolution process (see Section 3)

---

## 2. Refund Policy

### 2.1 Eligible Refund Scenarios

**Full Refund (100%)**:
- Mutual cancellation by both parties
- Service not delivered by payee
- Goods not received by payer
- Technical error in transaction processing
- Duplicate payment made

**Partial Refund**:
- Partial delivery of goods/services (as agreed by both parties)
- Quality issues resolved through negotiation

**No Refund**:
- Service/goods delivered as agreed
- Payer's change of mind after delivery
- TrustPay service fees (1-2% transaction fee)

### 2.2 Refund Process

1. **Request Submission**: Submit refund request through dashboard or email support@trustpay.in
2. **Verification**: We verify the transaction details and reason for refund (1-2 business days)
3. **Approval**: If approved, refund is initiated
4. **Processing**: Refund processed to original UPI account
5. **Completion**: Funds credited within 3-5 business days

### 2.3 Refund Timeline

| Scenario | Timeline |
|----------|----------|
| Mutual cancellation | 3-5 business days |
| Dispute resolution | 7-14 business days |
| Technical error | 1-3 business days |
| Failed transaction | Instant to 24 hours |

---

## 3. Dispute Resolution

### 3.1 When to Raise a Dispute
- Goods/services not delivered
- Quality issues
- Disagreement on delivery terms
- Fraudulent activity suspected

### 3.2 Dispute Process

1. **Raise Dispute**: Click "Raise Dispute" on escrow transaction
2. **Provide Evidence**: Upload proof (screenshots, messages, receipts)
3. **Review Period**: Both parties can submit their case (3-5 days)
4. **Resolution**: TrustPay team reviews and makes a decision (5-7 days)
5. **Outcome**: Funds released to appropriate party or refunded

### 3.3 Dispute Resolution Criteria
- Evidence provided by both parties
- Communication history
- Delivery proof
- Terms agreed upon at escrow creation
- Platform terms of service

## 5. Failed Transactions

### 5.1 Payment Failures
- If UPI payment fails, no amount is deducted
- If amount is deducted but transaction fails, **automatic refund** within 24 hours
- If refund not received, contact support with transaction ID

### 5.2 Release Failures
- If fund release to payee fails, we retry automatically
- If multiple attempts fail, funds are held securely
- Payee must update UPI details or contact support

---

## 6. Refund Methods

### 6.1 UPI Refund (Primary)
- Refunds processed to the **original UPI account** used for payment
- Fastest method: 3-5 business days
- No additional charges

### 6.2 Bank Transfer (Alternative)
- If UPI refund fails, bank transfer initiated
- Requires bank account details verification
- Processing time: 5-7 business days

### 6.3 Wallet Credit (Optional)
- Users can opt for TrustPay wallet credit
- Instant credit
- Can be used for future transactions

---

## 7. Special Circumstances

### 7.1 Fraudulent Transactions
- Suspected fraud cases are investigated immediately
- Funds frozen until investigation complete
- Refund processed if fraud confirmed
- Legal action may be taken against fraudulent parties

### 7.2 Technical Issues
- Platform downtime or technical errors
- Full refund with no service fee
- Expedited processing: 1-3 business days

### 7.3 Force Majeure
- Natural disasters, government actions, or other uncontrollable events
- Refunds processed as soon as possible
- Timeline may be extended beyond standard periods
